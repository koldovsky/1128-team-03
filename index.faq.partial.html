Welcome to Healthy Pet

We do our best to keep your pets healthy. We bring to your attention the answers to the main questions that are most often asked by the clients of our clinic.

Do you have a license?

Certainly. Our veterinary clinic has all the necessary licenses and permits. We provide them upon request.

What kind of animals do you treat?

Our staff consists of veterinarians of various profiles. We treat almost all animals, from cats and dogs to exotic species of parrots.

Is home visit possible?

A home visit is possible only in case of emergency, if it is dangerous to transport the animal to our clinic.

How are payments made for your services?

You can pay for our services in any convenient way. We accept cash, cashless payments, and credit cards.
